## Content priority queue

Based on your cluster, pick the first 3 pieces to write. Use this order as a starting point:

1. **Bottom-of-funnel first:** A comparison, review, or "best X for Y" piece — converts readers most directly.
2. **FAQ/informational second:** Answers a real question your audience has — earns AI citations.
3. **Off-page third:** Written natively for LinkedIn, Reddit, or Quora — builds brand mentions and links.
