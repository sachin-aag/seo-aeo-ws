# Content Rewriter Prompt

Copy everything below the line and paste it into any LLM. Fill in the bracketed sections.

---

I have existing content that isn't getting the traffic or AI citations it should. Please restructure it for citability and clarity. Don't change the voice or the substance — change the structure.

**Primary keyword this piece targets:** [keyword or topic]

**Target audience:** [who reads this]

**Content to restructure:**

[PASTE YOUR CONTENT HERE]

---

Please apply these structural changes:

1. **Add a TLDR at the top** — 2–3 sentences that answer the main question directly. No preamble. No "In this article we will..."

2. **Rewrite vague H2s** — any heading that's too short, too generic, or doesn't stand alone as a useful statement needs to be rewritten. A good H2 answers a question on its own.
   - Before: "Why This Matters"
   - After: "Why poorly structured content doesn't get cited by AI tools"

3. **Break dense paragraphs** — any paragraph with more than one main claim should be split into single-claim sentences. The claim needs to land in a sentence by itself.

4. **Add or improve the FAQ** — if there's no FAQ section, add one at the end with 4–5 questions. If there is one, make sure each answer starts with a direct response in the first sentence.

5. **Fix the title if it's vague** — if the H1 doesn't contain the primary keyword in the first three words, or makes no specific promise, offer two improved alternatives.

**Rules:**
- Keep the original voice — don't make it sound like someone else wrote it
- Keep all specific examples, data points, and personal stories
- Don't add filler to make it longer
- Don't cut substance to make it shorter

**Output:**
- A brief change log (max 6 bullets) explaining what you changed and why
- The full restructured content
