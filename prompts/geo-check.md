# GEO Check Prompt

Copy everything below the line and paste it into any LLM. Fill in the bracketed sections.

---

I want to check my AI visibility — how my business appears in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and other AI search tools.

**Business name:** [your business name]

**What you do:** [one sentence — who you help and how]

**Business category:** [e.g. "UX design agency", "vegan meal prep delivery", "freelance copywriter"]

**City / region:** [where you serve, or "global / online" if not location-based]

**Website:** [your domain]

---

Please give me:

1. **6 exact queries to test** — queries I should paste into ChatGPT (with web search on) and Perplexity to check if I appear. Cover these categories:
   - Direct category + location ("best [category] in [city]")
   - Problem-first query ("how to find a good [category]")
   - Comparison query ("[category] vs [alternative]")
   - Direct brand mention ("[my business name]")

2. **A recording template** — a simple table I can fill in for each query/tool combination:
   - Mentioned? Yes / No / Partially
   - Citation URL (if cited)
   - Who shows up instead
   - Notes

3. **What the results mean** — for each possible outcome (mentioned + cited / mentioned but not cited / not mentioned), tell me what it means and what I should do next.

4. **30-day follow-up plan** — what should I do after running this check, and when should I check again to measure progress?
