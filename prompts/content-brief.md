# Content Brief Prompt

Copy everything below the line and paste it into any LLM. Fill in the bracketed sections.

---

I want to write a piece of content that ranks in search and gets cited by AI tools. Please create a full content brief for me.

**Target keyword or topic:** [what you want this piece to rank for]

**Search intent:** [Informational / Transactional / Commercial / Navigational — or describe what the searcher wants to do]

**Target audience:** [who is this written for?]

**Competing content I want to beat (optional):** [paste URLs of top-ranking pages on this topic, or describe what they cover]

**My unique angle or experience (optional):** [anything you know from experience that most content on this topic misses]

---

Please produce a complete content brief including:

1. **3-sentence summary** — what this piece is about, who it's for, and why it's worth reading

2. **Primary keyword + secondary keywords** — the main keyword plus 3–5 related terms to include naturally throughout

3. **TLDR draft** — write the 2–3 sentence TLDR that should appear at the top of the finished article. It must answer the main question directly in the first sentence — no preamble.

4. **Suggested H1** — one option, keyword in the first three words, specific promise, under 65 characters

5. **H2 outline** — the full structure of the piece, with each H2 being specific and descriptive (not vague like "Overview"), plus a one-sentence note on what each section covers

6. **FAQ section** — 5 questions a real reader would have, each with a draft direct answer (first sentence answers the question — no meandering)

7. **Content notes** — any specific data points, common misconceptions to address, or personal experience angles that would make this piece stand out

8. **Recommended length and format** — word count range and whether this should be a how-to guide, comparison, FAQ post, explainer, etc.
