# Backlink Gap Prompt

Copy everything below the line and paste it into any LLM. Fill in the bracketed sections.

---

I want to find backlink opportunities by identifying where my competitors are being cited and I'm not.

**My website:** [yourdomain.com]

**What I do:** [one sentence]

**Competitors (2–3 sites that outrank me):** 
- [competitor1.com]
- [competitor2.com]
- [competitor3.com — optional]

**My niche / industry:** [e.g. "B2B marketing tools", "personal finance for freelancers", "sustainable fashion"]

---

Please give me:

1. **Step-by-step instructions to run a free backlink gap analysis** — using tools that don't require a paid account (Ahrefs free checker, Google search operators, or similar). Give me the exact steps and what to look for.

2. **The gap categories most worth targeting for my niche** — based on what I've told you about my business, where are the most likely gap opportunities? Cover:
   - Directories (relevant ones, not link farms)
   - Roundup articles / "best of" lists
   - Guest post opportunities
   - Podcast show notes
   - Resource pages

3. **Three outreach email templates** — one for each of the most likely gap types. Templates should be:
   - Short (under 100 words)
   - Specific (reference the actual article or page, not generic)
   - Not asking for a favour — offering value or just pointing out the gap
   - Human, not corporate

4. **What NOT to do** — the backlink tactics that will get me penalised, not ranked. I don't want to waste time or damage my domain.

5. **Local SEO note** — if my business serves a local market, what local citation and directory actions should I prioritise first?
